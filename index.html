One identity · five feels

Five directions, one V2.

Every model below now wears the same V2 identity — the V2 wordmark, one geometric typeface (Jost) with Inter, and a single ivory / near-black / sky-blue palette. What changes between them is the architecture: density, scale, motion, and how the photography is framed. Each card is a live, scrollable homepage — the hero film plays once, and the interlude scrubs as you scroll. Click any one to open it full-screen.
